About Me

Ever since writing code in DOS on a Radio Shack Tandy PC in the 1980's, my interest in technology has blossomed into a life-long passion. After 15 years as an Interior Designer and Muralist, I decided to make 2018 a year of growth and enrichment by becoming a Full-Stack Web Developer. This led me to Galvanize's Immersive Web Development Program. I'm thrilled by all the possibilities that abound in fusing art & technology together.

One emerging technology that excites me is the application of machine learning to aging-in-place products for the elderly. This is one of the technological sectors where my web-dev expertise could coalesce with my extensive interior design background. Another area that really ignites my internal flame is the design and fabrication of immersive art; the beautiful dance between form and function yields so much room for innovation. Regardless of the position I’m in, I know I will be able to apply my versatile knowledge-base and overcome the task at hand.

In my spare time when I'm not coding, you'll usually find me doing something creative. I'm a muralist, theatrical prop maker, a costume designer & a fire-spinning tribal belly dancer! I thrive when exploring things that I’ve never tried before; adventure is the spice of life!

I would like to find a socially conscious company where I can learn from other developers & designers while contributing my creative and technical skills to the team. If you have a professional opportunity for which I might be a good fit, please don’t hesitate to contact me! You can also check out my latest projects on GitHub and connect with me on LinkedIn.

Dev Projects

Personafi

An ecommerce marketplace that sells a variety of personality modules for voice assisted devices. Created with React, Next.js, Moltin & AWS

Urban Canvas

Look up or post street art in your area or the locations you visit. A full stack app created with React, node, express, knex, & postgresql. This was a group project. I was responsible for the app concept, layout & styling of the splash page & pair programming to build the back end.

Show Me Your Kitties!

A full stack app that allows the user to post pics & info of your favorite kitties. Created with React, Semantic UI, Node, Express, Knex, & Postgresql

Cave Plan

A front end app that uses an api to allow the user to view Paleo recipes & plan there menu for the week. Created with html, css, javascript, bootstrap & Yummly api
